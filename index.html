Racing for the Senate

With Republicans holding 24 of the 34 seats up for re-election in November, Democrats will be looking to win back the control of the Senate they lost in 2014. Currently at 46 seats, they will need to win four more to gain control if a Democrat is elected president, or five more if a Republican wins the White House.
